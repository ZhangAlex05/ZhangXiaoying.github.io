Who am I ？

I am Xiaoying Zhang, is an international student at the Hague University of applied science studying User Experience Design. I value the users' values to design. I insist on empathizing with users, using design methods to find problems, solve problems, output design solutions, and realize the improvement of customer products and enterprise value.

What can I do for you ？

As a UX designer, I design to improve the user experience of using the product. User experience design improves user satisfaction by improving the usability, ease of use, and pleasure of human interaction with the product. User experience design is included in human-computer interaction design and extends to solve all problems related to user experience.I am responsible for producing innovative, visually appealing human interface designs; creating design prototypes; working with developers to make designs a reality. I can work independently and engage team members. Design services include interactive prototype design, product visual design, website design and development, mobile and software product interface design, icon design, brand, graphic design, etc.

Design Thinking Process

UXD

1

Promote a Theme

Step 1 Promote a theme to stimulate creative solutions.

2

Research User

Step 2 User analysis gathering data to research the user and to discover what the user wants by empathizing with users. I'll show you what kind of user research I've done, and research existing problems. This is a collect and research data process, and an objective facts presentation.

3

Define Questions

Step 3 Define questions to conduct all research findings to form the results Extract useful information and sort out design problems. I will conduct in-depth analysis and sorting of the survey data, screen and collect the useful parts, and analyze them. Using some tools to define problems, I will gradually deduce the problems that need to be solved in the design.

4

Identify Questions

Step 4 Identify questions to find out the core questions and formulate the design direction. Every user analysis and product research will dig out many Pain points of users. In this step, I will prioritize the problems summarize the points that I need to focus on this project identify the design problems, and a final client brief.

5

Develop Concepts

Step 5 Develop concepts and design proposals to drive solutions with developing ideation. It's divided into two parts. The first half is the phase of development, in which I explore and try solutions through subjective decisions and design discussions. The focus of this phase is to gradually sort out the design process and quickly produce multiple design solutions for trial and error.

6

Prototyping & test

Step 6 Prototyping and test to structure the framework of design by prototypes and to optimize the design by testing the prototypes. I will build prototypes, including paper prototypes, low-fidelity prototypes, and high-fidelity prototypes, to present and express design ideas. With the progress of user testing and prototype iteration, the results of the design are gradually detailed, and the design scheme is constantly optimized according to the needs of users until the project gets satisfactory results.

7

Design Presence
